About

"We will not falter; we shall not fall; so long as the shadows crawl."

~ Nightwatch Creed

As the sun sets, the trusted toys of the watch begin their nightly duty, ready to fight against the forces of darkness and fear to protect their child. Armed with flashlights and full of hope, the heroic toys must make their way through the dark to destroy the root of their child’s fear.

Nightwatch is a 2.5D platformer about the unbreakable bonds that exist between a child and their toys. Players take on the role of one child’s toys, working to protect the boy as he slumbers. Explore a warped bedroom while fighting creatures from the darkness with light and discover what it truly means to be a hero.

Characters

Characters

Barry

The child’s first toy, Barry has been through everything, from the first day of school to sandboxes and the occasional laundry machine when the child isn’t looking. As the unofficial leader of the Nightwatch, the stuffed bear tends to order the group around much to the others’ distain.

Rex

The newest member of the watch, Rex was acquired from a museum trip with the child’s grandparents. Having spent so much time in a place of learning the short armed reptile acquired a bit of an encyclopedic knowledge, able to recite interesting facts at will. Though he’s not the bravest warrior on the watch, he’s more than willing to help protect the child who gave him a home.

Cubert

A master of construction, demolition and getting sucked up by the vacuum, Cubert is the watch’s resident mechanic. Thanks to his patented cataswing technology, the group is able to fire off a multitude of batteries for them to use out in the darkness. With his mix of contraptions, this is definitely one toy the bad guys don’t want to mess with.

About Us

We are an independent game studio made up of students from Fanshawe College. To us, games are a way to be able to explore boundaries outside of what is possible with other mediums. Whether it’s designing functionality or modeling a character, we love learning and seeing how all these components blend together to create an interesting world. While this is homework for us, we sincerely hope that you as player enjoy the fruits of our labour!
